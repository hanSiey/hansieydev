Details

This current version of the website released is an upgrade from the initial project which started October 2021. The initial release was a basic 4 page website that allowed users to place orders online. Now it is a functional e-commerce website that enables viewers to browse through the products and shop online. Customers can now be registered users that have a profile with all past orders, all customers registered or not will be notified by email automatically when their order status is updated. The upgrade also comes with a built-in customized administraion panel only accessible by the admin. In the panel the admin has full control over products desplayed, customers, orders and all messages sent through the website. This upgrade does not include online payments and ssl yet. The project is continuously under development, upgrades and new technologies are still to come.
